Contact

The Human Welfare Research Framework welcomes serious engagement from researchers, scholars within the wisdom traditions, sustainable-development practitioners, and institutional partners with an interest in the framework's regional or methodological dimensions.

For correspondence, please write to:

rajiv@humanwelfareresearch.org

We aim to respond to substantive enquiries within seven working days. Brevity in your initial note is welcome — a sentence or two on who you are and the nature of your interest is enough.

What kinds of engagement we welcome

  • Scholars within any of the ten traditions catalogued in the framework, willing to confirm or correct specific citations in our principle database.
  • Researchers in sustainable-development, regional ecology, or community-led conservation interested in the methodology or in the design of Project Hoshiarpur.
  • Institutional partners — academic, governmental, or non-governmental — interested in the framework's verification structure.
  • Critique. The project is at the stage where considered critique improves the work more than affirmation does.

What we are not yet able to do

  • Accept research collaborators on a paid or formal-affiliation basis.
  • Provide grants, funding, or stipends.
  • Offer letters of recommendation or institutional endorsement.
  • Confirm participation in conferences, panels, or other public engagements outside the project's pre-pilot scope.

The framework is currently led by a single principal investigator with two AI partners, working under transparent collaboration discipline. The team will expand as the work matures and as formal institutional engagement is consolidated.

We read every substantive enquiry. We may not respond instantly — the principal investigator works under a parallel professional commitment — but we will respond within the timeframe noted above. If a message has gone unanswered after that period, a brief gentle follow-up is welcome.
